# iFit 3.0.0 — Google Play Store Listing

## App Title
iFit — Workout Planner, Training Log & Progress Tracker

## Short Description
Structured strength training, workout logging, exercise guidance, and progress analytics in one polished app.

## Full Description
Build strength, stay consistent, and track every rep with iFit. Version 3.0.0 introduces the complete training experience for lifters who want a simple, performance-first workout app.

### What the app does
- Guides your training with a 6-day strength training split and rest days.
- Lets you start a workout, log sets, update weights/reps, and mark exercises complete.
- Shows detailed exercise pages with muscle focus, equipment, step-by-step form cues, and animated exercise demonstrations.
- Tracks your workout history, training streak, weekly volume, personal records, and bodyweight progress.
- Helps you browse and search a curated exercise library by muscle group, equipment, and difficulty.

### How the app works
- The Workout tab shows today’s session and lets you start or resume a workout with one tap.
- The Library tab provides an easy way to search exercises, filter by muscle group, and inspect every movement.
- The Progress tab turns your logged sessions into actionable analytics: streaks, weekly load, PR count, strength trends, and bodyweight charts.
- Each exercise screen explains why the movement is included, highlights primary and secondary muscles, and displays training program context.
- Workouts are saved automatically, so your progress history builds as you train.

### App goal
iFit is designed to help serious lifters and disciplined gym-goers stay consistent with their training plan while keeping progress tracking simple and focused.

This version is built for users who want:
- a ready-made strength training schedule,
- clear exercise guidance,
- a fast workout logging flow,
- and meaningful performance feedback without the clutter.

### Key features
- Daily workout plan with push/pull/legs structure and dedicated rest days
- Easy start/continue workout flow with exercise-by-exercise logging
- Exercise library with searchable movements, equipment tags, and difficulty labels
- Progress analytics with charts for volume, frequency, bodyweight, and strength
- Workout history for completed sessions with session stats
- Personal record tracking and exercise-specific progress graphs
- Warmup/stretch guidance built into the program structure

### Why users will love it
- Clean, no-nonsense interface built for fast gym use
- Balanced training plan focused on compound lifts, accessory work, and recovery
- Powerful tracking that turns every session into useful progress data
- Built to support both beginners and experienced lifters who want better daily consistency
